MEDICAL PURPOSE OF BECOMING MORE FIT

If it wasnt already widely known exercise has a lot of health benefits. you would be more likely to live a healthier lifestyke if you stay fit. Here are the reasons

  1. To improve one's blood circulation= When your body performs a certain exerting activity, the blood flow is usually increased to help oxygen reach the muscles and keep your body stable
  2. To help with muscle mass increase for future activiy of the same or different but equally exerting type= When you perform that workout, for example squats when lifting a heavy weight, your muscle mass on the exerted area increases. This is usually more gradual and works over a longer time but the changes can be seen soon enough when similar tasks grow easier with time. Quite helpful isnt it? For bigger growth, heavier and more exerting exercise is needed. Which is why there are all sorts of heavy and forcefully exerting equipment at a gym
  3. To prevent common lifestyle diseases which affect most people= The reason why high blood pressure, diabetes and cardiac issues isnt always related to genetics or exposure. A lot of the times, it can be as a result of poor sedantary habits.
