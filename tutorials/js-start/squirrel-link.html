Squirrels

Squirrels are commonly thought of tree-dwelling mammals, but the squirrel family extends far beyond that to include ground-dwelling rodents rodents such as chipmunks and prairie dogs, and flying squirrels.

Two of the most common and best-known squirrel species are the:

  • Red squirrel
  • Eastern gray squirrel
